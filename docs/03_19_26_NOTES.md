# Personal Development Notes

## Creative Cycle

A repeating pattern:

1. Work deeply on a project.
2. Hit a conceptual wall.
3. Step away to another project.
4. Learn something new.
5. Return with clarity and make major progress.

This is not a weakness — it's a sustainable creative process.

## Strengths Observed

- Ability to design systems from first principles.
- Strong intuition for architecture.
- Willingness to experiment and iterate.
- Healthy instinct to step away when stuck.
- Natural cross-training between projects.

## Long-Term Growth

- Year 1: foundational understanding.
- Year 3–4: architectural clarity.
- Year 5–6: mastery of language/runtime design.
